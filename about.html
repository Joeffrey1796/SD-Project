Our Story

Crafting coffee moments that bring warmth and connection.

From Beans to Brew

At Coffee Haven, we believe that every cup tells a story. Our journey began with a simple dream — to create a place where people could enjoy handcrafted coffee made with passion and care. Inspired by local roasters and global coffee traditions, we blend artistry and sustainability in every brew.

We source only the finest ethically grown beans, roasted to perfection to bring out rich, aromatic flavors. Whether you prefer your coffee bold or smooth, we have something crafted just for you.

Step inside our café, take a sip, and feel at home — because at Coffee Haven, comfort is served with every cup.

Our Mission

At Sideway’s Café, our mission is to create a warm and inviting space where every cup tells a story. We strive to serve high-quality, sustainably sourced coffee while fostering community connection and inspiring creativity in every sip.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become a local favorite and a symbol of comfort — where passion for coffee meets genuine hospitality, and every guest feels at home.
